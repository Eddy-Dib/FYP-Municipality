# FYP: Municipality Management System

## **Introduction**
This project proposes the development of an automated Municipality Management System designed to digitalize and streamline core municipal services. The system provides a centralized platform for citizens, municipal employees and administrators to manage requests, documents, tasks and communications efficiently.

The goal of the platform is to replace traditional paper-based processes with a structured digital system that improves accessibility, transparency and operational efficiency within municipal administrations.


## **Problem Statement**
Municipality services are often managed through manual, paper-based processes. These traditional workflows frequently result in:
  - Long service processing times
  - Poor coordination between departments
  - Limited transparency for citizens
  - Risk of document loss or administrative errors
  - Inefficient communication between the municipality and the public

Manual systems also make it difficult to track requests, monitor employee workloads and maintain accurate records.
A digital system with automated workflows, validation mechanisms and centralized data storage can significantly reduce these issues.


## **Objectives**
The objective of this project is to design and implement a web-based municipality management system that improves the efficiency and transparency of municipal services.

The system aims to:
- Digitalize citizen service requests and complaints
- Improve task management and coordination between employees
- Provide transparency and tracking for citizens
- Reduce administrative workload through automation
- Maintain secure and structured records of municipal operations


## **Proposed Solution**
The proposed solution is a web-based application that centralizes municipal operations within a single digital platform.
The system introduces role-based access for different types of users, allowing citizens, employees and administrators to interact with the system according to their responsibilities.
Through automated request handling, task assignment, document management and reporting tools, the platform improves communication between departments while making municipal services more accessible to citizens.


## **Core Functionalities**

### Citizen Portal
Citizens can access a personal account where they can:
  - Submit service requests
  - Submit complaints to the municipality
  - Upload supporting documents
  - Track request and complaint status
  - View municipal announcements and events
  - View outstanding fees and payment history
  - Receive notifications regarding request updates or unpaid fees

### Employee Portal
Municipality employees have role-based access to system functionalities.

Employees can:
- View and manage assigned tasks
- Review request details
- Update task and request statuses
- Submit reports related to requests or inspections
- Upload supporting documents
- Escalate tasks to supervisors or other departments
- Track task history and activity

Specialized employees may also perform additional operations such as scheduling inspections or validating permits.

### Requests and Complaint Management System
The system provides a structured workflow for handling citizen submissions.

Features include:
  - Creation of service requests and complaints
  - Tracking of request statuses (Pending, In Progress, Completed, Rejected)
  - Priority management
  - Assignment of tasks to employees
  - Internal reporting and document submission
  - Escalation of requests when required

### Admin Panel
Administrators are responsible for system supervision and configuration.

The admin panel allows administrators to:
  - Manage employees and assign roles
  - Monitor system activity
  - Oversee requests and complaints
  - Reassign tasks if necessary
  - Manage system announcements and municipal events
  - View system activity logs

### Finance Management
Finance employees can manage municipality fees and payment records.

Capabilities include:
  - Setting or updating municipal fee amounts
  - Reviewing payment records
  - Monitoring unpaid fees
  - Sending alerts or notifications for overdue payments

### Inspection and Permit Management
Certain requests may require technical or legal verification.

Engineers can:
  - Review construction-related requests
  - Schedule inspections
  - Submit inspection reports

Lawyers can:
  - Review permit-related requests
  - Validate legal compliance
  - Approve or reject permits based on municipal regulations

### Document Management
The system provides secure document handling, including:
  - Uploading supporting documents for requests and complaints
  - Storing documents in a centralized system
  - Controlled access based on user roles
  - Document tracking and validation


### Reporting and Analytics
The system supports administrative analysis through reporting tools.

Reports may include:
  - Request processing statistics
  - Employee performance metrics
  - Department workload analysis
  - Request resolution rates over time
